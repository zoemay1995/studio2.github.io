The organization I chose as inspiration for this map is the Human Rights Campaign.
Their campaign uses a great color scheme (a Navy blue and a bold yellow) that catches
your eye when you see it. I went to their website
and copied an image of their logo. I then went to this website
and retrieved the hex codes relating to the colors of the logo. I used pink for landuse as it stands out
against the blue and the yellow and is clearly visible. For mountain ranges I used a dark blue/dark green to stand out against the yellow land color.
